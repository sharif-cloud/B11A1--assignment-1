Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Tech and creative ideas will drive progress. Startups and research will get support. Smart solutions will shape the future.

Sustainability

Eco-friendly growth is the goal. Green energy and recycling will be key. Future-ready and safe environment for all.

Infrastructure

Smart cities and transport will expand. Rural-urban links will improve. Modern roads, bridges, and internet access.

Empowerment

Everyone will get equal chances. Focus on youth, women, and the underprivileged. Skill-building and entrepreneurship will rise.
